Welecome to My College

Marwari College was established in the year 1963 by the Marwari Shiksha Trust to facilitate higher education for the young and promising students of society, specially the tribes and downtrodden. This premier college of Jharkhand State came into existence with sincere and incessant efforts of Late Ganga Prasad Budhia.

Presently, Marwari College, Ranchi, as an Autonomous Institution, has got NAAC Accredition for Higher Studies having 'Potential for Excellence' Award given by UGC, is providing Undergraduate and Post-Graduate Regular Courses to the students. It is also providing Under Graduate and Post-Graduate Job Oriented Courses.
